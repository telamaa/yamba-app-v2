## 2. About the platform

Yamba is a peer-to-peer marketplace for shipping light parcels, connecting:

- **Shippers** who wish to send parcels
- **Carriers** (also called "Trippers") who accept to carry parcels during their trips

Yamba does not perform the transport itself. It acts as a technical intermediary facilitating the connection between users.

## 4. How the platform works

### 4.1 Publishing a trip

Carriers can publish a trip with:

- The transport mode (plane, train, car)
- Origin and destination
- Departure and arrival times
- Accepted parcel categories
- Pricing conditions

### 4.2 Shipping request

Shippers can search for a matching trip and send a request to the carrier. The carrier is free to accept or decline.

### 4.3 Payment

Payments are processed through Stripe Connect. Yamba charges a commission on each transaction. Fee details are displayed at booking time.
