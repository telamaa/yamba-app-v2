## 3. Data collected

## 4. Purposes of processing

Yamba processes your personal data for:

- **Account creation and management**: authentication, access security

- **Payments**: transaction processing via Stripe Connect

- **Security**: fraud, abuse, and dispute prevention
- **Service improvement**: statistical analysis, UX optimization
- **Legal compliance**: accounting, tax, and anti-money-laundering obligations

- **Legal obligations** (Art. 6.1.c GDPR): for accounting record retention

## 9. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access**: obtain a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact: **dpo@yamba.com**
